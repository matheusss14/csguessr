Who are ya? Counter-Strike is a Counter-Strike 2 quiz game

  • You have 5 chances to guess the Counter-Strike 2 player from the most known teams from all around the world
  • After each guess, feedback will be revealed, showing how close your guess is to the mystery CS player in a number of categories.
  • Green bubbles means that that category is spot on, while a yellow bubble means the guess was close in that category, but not quite right. A blank/white bubble means it is not correct.
  • Example guess: David 'frozen' Cernansky
    FaZe
    Europe
    Rifler
    21
    4
    1.13
    Team Nation Region Role Age Major apps 2024 Rating
    ... Means that the mystery player is European and does not play for FaZe. However, the player is also a Rifler and they are over the age of 21 whilst having a lower number of major appearances than 4.
    The correct answer would be Justinas 'jL' Lekavicius.
  • Nation stands for the players' nationality while Region stands for the teams' region. For example, cadiaN would have his nation as Denmark, but his region would be NA, as he currently plays for Team Liquid.
